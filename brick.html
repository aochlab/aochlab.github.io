Brick

Location: trench 1, context 3

Brick was one of the most abundant materials on our site. This is the collection of brick found just in the third context of trench 1, but we collected hundreds of pieces of brick in each of our four trenches. This is indicative of the large amount of construction that occurred on our site — both 58 College St’s destruction in 1969 and the multi-year construction project that was building the List Art Building. According to a 1940 Residential Assessment Record of 58 College St, the house had 3 chimneys, which were all almost certainly made of brick. Thus, it is reasonable to assume that the destruction of the building would have left residual brick around our site. Further, construction of the List Art Building most likely involved significant quantities of brick as well. It is impossible to determine whether the brick we found is associated with 58 College St or with the List Art Building, but it is highly likely that it is a mixture of brick from both buildings.
